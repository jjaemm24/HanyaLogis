Silicon Nitride Ceramics
Product Introduction:

Silicon nitride ceramic parts are made of high-purity silicon nitride powder sintered at high temperature. They are usually used to make parts in harsh environments such as high temperature, high pressure, and corrosion, such as high-temperature turbine blades, high-pressure pump valves, and corrosion-resistant pump bodies.

Product Features:
  • High strength and hardness: The hardness of silicon nitride ceramic parts is as high as 9.5 on the Mohs scale, so they have good wear resistance.
  • The melting point of silicon nitride ceramic parts is 2000°C, and they have stable properties in high temperature environments.
  • Corrosion resistance: Silicon nitride ceramic parts can resist corrosion from various acids, alkalis, salts and other media.
  • Good biocompatibility.
Application Fields:
  • Aerospace: such as engine blades, turbine blades, etc.
  • Automobile manufacturing: such as valves, pump bodies, etc.
  • Electronics: such as capacitors, resistors, etc.
