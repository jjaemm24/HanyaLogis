Medical Ceramics
Product Introduction:

Zirconia medical ceramic parts are made of high-purity zirconium oxide powder sintered at high temperature. This kind of ceramic parts have the characteristics of high strength, high hardness, high wear resistance, corrosion resistance, high temperature resistance, etc., so they are widely used in many important occasions in the medical field. For example, they are used to make medical instruments such as scalpels, surgical forceps, hemostats, bone drills, artificial joints, etc.

Product Features:
  • High strength and high hardness: The hardness of zirconia medical ceramic parts is as high as Mohs hardness 7.5 and has good wear resistance.
  • High corrosion resistance: It can resist corrosion from various acids, alkalis, salts and other media.
  • High temperature resistance: The melting point is 1600°C, and it has strong high temperature resistance and can be used in high temperature environments.
  • Biocompatibility: Ceramic materials have good compatibility with organisms and will not have adverse eﬀects on the human body.
Application Fields:
  • Surgical instruments: used to make medical instruments such as scalpels, surgical forceps, hemostats, bone drills, artiﬁcial joints, etc.
  • Diagnostic equipment: used to make parts in diagnostic equipment such as X-ray machines and CT machines, thereby improving the quality and performance of the equipment.
  • Rehabilitation equipment: used to make parts in rehabilitation equipment, such as artiﬁcial knees, artiﬁcial hip joints, etc.
Semiconductor Ceramics
Product Introduction:

Semiconductor ceramics are a new type of semiconductor material with high electrical conductivity, low thermal conductivity and high thermal stability. They are usually used to make electronic devices in harsh environments such as high temperature, high pressure and high frequency.

Product Features:
  • Good high temperature stability, melting point up to 1300℃.
  • Low thermal conductivity.
  • High electrical conductivity.
  • Good biocompatibility.
Application Fields:
  • High-temperature electronic devices: such as high-temperature superconduc ng devices, plasma devices, etc.
  • High-voltage electronic devices: such as high-voltage accelerators, plasma beam equipment, etc.
  • High-frequency electronic devices: such as high-frequency ﬁlters, radio frequency communica on equipment, etc.
  • Bioelectronic devices: such as biosensors, biomedical equipment, etc.
Electronic Structural Ceramics
Product Introduction:

Electronic structural ceramics are a new type of ceramic material. This material has excellent physical and chemical properties and is widely used in electronics, communications, aerospace, medical equipment and other ﬁelds.

Product Features:
  • Excellent electrical properties: Electronic structural ceramics have excellent electrical insulation, low dielectric constant, high dielectric strength, low loss and other characteristics, and can work stably in harsh environments such as high frequency, high voltage and high temperature.
  • High hardness and high strength: Electronic structural ceramics can withstand high-intensity mechanical stress and chemical corrosion.
  • Good precision processing: Electronic structural ceramics are suitable for being manufactured into high-precision ceramic parts and devices.
Application Fields:
  • Electronic components: widely used in communications, computers, consumer electronics and other ﬁelds.
  • Aerospace: electronic structural ceramics can be used to manufacture high-precision, high-strength aerospace components.
  • Medical devices: electronic structural ceramics can be used to manufacture high-precision medical devices, such as artiﬁcial joints, implants, etc.
